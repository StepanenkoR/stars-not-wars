# 🛰️ Stars Not Wars

[Читати українською 🇺🇦](README.uk.md)

> Mad old men have hijacked too much of human history. They turn nations into weapons, burn trillions on war, and call it order while killing the future in front of us. We refuse that logic. We are here for life — happy, meaningful, still-unlived life. Life where you do not kill, but build. Life where you explore, share, and create instead of destroying, lying, and stealing. The goal is not to die in their games. The goal is to outgrow them and build what comes next.

## The Vision
This project is a manifest against the irrationality of the modern world. While humanity is held hostage by the ambitions of out-of-touch leaders obsessed with the wars of the past, we choose a different path.

We believe that the era of mad old men fighting for borders must end. It is time to redirect our collective intelligence from destruction to expansion.

### Our Goals:
1. **Dyson Swarm Design:** Creating an open-source roadmap for a civilization that harvests the full power of its star.
2. **Radical Life Extension:** Investing in the biology of the future, not the weaponry of the past.

### How to Join?
There are no requirements for your background, degree, or location.  
It is enough to believe in one simple truth:  
**Mad old men must stop burning our future in wars and let us finally build what comes next.**

If you have ideas, code, or just a vision of a world where we build megastructures instead of trenches — you are welcome here.
