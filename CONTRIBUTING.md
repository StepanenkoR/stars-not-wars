# 🤝 Contributing to Stars Not Wars

First of all, thank you for being here! This project is driven by the idea that human intelligence belongs to the stars, not to the trenches.

## How can you help?
You don't need to be a Senior Developer or a NASA engineer to contribute. We value:
- **Ideas:** Share your vision of space expansion or life extension.
- **Calculations:** Help us build Python scripts to model the Dyson Swarm or medical funding shifts.
- **Translations:** Help us translate the manifesto into more languages.
- **Discussions:** Participate in our "Discussions" tab to shape the roadmap.

## Our Philosophy
We focus on the future of humanity as a biological and cosmic species. We leave the conflicts of the past behind. Be respectful, be bold, and use AI to augment your creativity.
